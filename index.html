Exploring Sacred Texts: Bible & Quran

A true understanding of faith's foundations blossoms when we explore the sacred texts that have guided billions across millennia. Here, we humbly present comparative passages, aiming to foster deeper insight, mutual respect, and a spirit of unity among diverse traditions. This study is offered in the spirit of shared learning and interfaith harmony, reflecting our unwavering commitment to uncovering universal truths.

Theme: The Divine Act of Creation

📜 Bible: Genesis - The Beginning

"In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth. Now the earth was formless and empty, darkness was over the surface of the deep, and the Spirit of God was hovering over the waters. And God said, 'Let there be light,' and there was light."

Genesis 1:1-3 (Referenced from KJV/NIV, explored via Blue Letter Bible)

📜 Quran: Al-A'raf - The Creation

"Indeed, your Lord is Allah, who created the heavens and earth in six periods and then established Himself above the Throne. He covers the night with the day, [another night] chasing it rapidly; and [He created] the sun, the moon, and the stars, subjected by His command..."

Quran 7:54 (Referenced from Sahih International, studied via Corpus Quran)

Theme: The Essence of Charity and Giving

💖 Bible: Teachings on Generosity

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver." (2 Corinthians 9:7, NIV)

"Bring the whole tithe into the storehouse, that there may be food in my house. Test me in this,” says the Lord Almighty, “and see if I will not throw open the floodgates of heaven and pour out so much blessing that there will not be room enough to store it.” (Malachi 3:10, NIV)

Biblical principles of stewardship, joyful giving, and divine blessing.

💖 Quran: Teachings on Zakat & Sadaqah

"And establish prayer and give zakah, and whatever good you put forward for yourselves - you will find it with Allah. Indeed, Allah of what you do, is Seeing." (Quran 2:110, Sahih International)

"Those who spend their wealth [in Allah's way] by night and by day, secretly and publicly - they will have their reward with their Lord. And no fear will there be concerning them, nor will they grieve." (Quran 2:274, Sahih International)

Quranic injunctions on obligatory and voluntary charity, and its spiritual rewards.
